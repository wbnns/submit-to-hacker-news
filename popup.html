HN Submission Guidelines

What to Submit:
  • On-Topic: Anything that gratifies intellectual curiosity
  • Off-Topic: Politics, crime, sports, celebrities, TV news content
Title Guidelines:
  • No uppercase, exclamation points, or promotional language
  • Remove site name (it will be shown automatically)
  • Use original title unless misleading or clickbait
  • Remove gratuitous numbers: "10 Ways" → "How To"
  • Add [video] or [pdf] tags when applicable
Submission Rules:
  • Submit original source, not reports about it
  • Don't use HN primarily for self-promotion
  • Don't delete and repost
  • Don't solicit votes or comments
